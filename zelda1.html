Unity Remake of Zelda1 Dungeon

Remade the first dungeon, Eagle, from NES Zelda 1, and built a brand-new massive level with new mechanisms and blocks! Dodge deadly projectiles—or use them against your enemies!

This project was developed by me (haoqianz@umich.edu) and my teammate Tianlin Huang (huangtl@umich.edu) using C# and the Unity engine. Art assets were drawn and processed in Photoshop. My primary responsibilities included developing all enemies, designing player and enemy attributes, implementing the inventory system, drop system, UI and HUD, audio manager, and most of the sound processing.

In the new level, I was responsible for developing the new mechanisms and blocks. This design introduced fresh mechanics, bringing brand-new gameplay and puzzle elements—while staying true to the retro pixel art style!
